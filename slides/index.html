📚BooksWithYou
Product Introduction

A social reading tracker for book clubs & friends

Create shared reading sessions, log chapter progress together, and discuss books in real time — all in one place.

Get Started → See Features
The Problem

Book clubs are messy.

📱

Scattered Channels

Group chats, emails, spreadsheets — nobody knows where the reading schedule lives.

❓

No Progress Visibility

Who has caught up? Who fell behind? No easy way to see where everyone is.

😶

Discussion Friction

Great insights get lost in chat scroll. No persistent place to discuss each book.

⏰

Accountability Drain

Without visible tracking, reading goals drift and clubs lose momentum.

The Solution

One place to read together.

BooksWithYou brings books, progress tracking, and discussion into a simple, beautiful experience.

📖

Create a Session

Pick a book, set chapters, and invite your reading circle.

📊

Track Progress

Log chapters as you go. See everyone's progress at a glance.

💬

Discuss & React

Share thoughts and react with emojis — live, in real time.

Features

Everything your reading group needs

🔒

Public & Private Sessions

Open to the world or invite-only — you decide who joins the read.

📑

Chapter-by-Chapter Tracking

Break books into chapters. Log progress with simple +/- controls.

⚡

Live Updates

Progress, comments, and reactions sync instantly via Supabase Realtime.

😊

Emoji Reactions

Celebrate milestones and react to comments with one click.
